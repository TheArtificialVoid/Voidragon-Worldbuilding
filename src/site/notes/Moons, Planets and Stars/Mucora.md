Murcora is the second planet found in the [[Planetary Systems/Mykesian System\|Mykesian System]]. Home to the infectious fungi-like worldeater [[Plants and Animals/Xejo-Xero\|Xejo-Xero]], the planet is under strict prevention law stopping any from visiting the entire solar system.
